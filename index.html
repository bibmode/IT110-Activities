▼ My childhood

My earliest childhood memories were when we were living in Iligan City. Our house was near the beach so, as a sapling, I get to play around the beach on Saturdays. I had a lot of fun pretending to be mermaids with my friends on the shore even when I didn't know how to swim. Looking back, I probably had an undiagnosed ADHD of some sort since I could not sit still as a kid. I was known to draw on the board and influence my friends to play around while our teacher is trying to teach us.

We moved to Butuan City when I was around 6 years old. I remembered feeling lonely after realizing that I lost all my friends from Iligan, but it came around fine because I get to make new ones here and adapted well in time. Still, my youngest and most carefree days in Iligan will remain with me forever.
